Privacy Policy for Sign&Send
Last Updated: 01/03/2025
1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how Sign&Send collects, uses, and shares information in
connection with your use of our desktop application designed for judicial executor
offices.
2. Information Collection
Our Application is designed to facilitate the sending of signed legal documents via
email using your Gmail account. To provide this functionality, we require access to:
• Gmail sending capabilities (Gmail Send scope)
• Gmail reading capabilities for attachment handling (Gmail Read scope)
3. OAuth Authentication
Our Application uses Google's OAuth 2.0 protocol to securely access your Gmail
account. This means:
• We never store, see, or have access to your Google account password
• You authenticate directly with Google, not with our Application
• You can revoke our Application's access at any time through your Google
Account settings
4. How We Use Information
Our Application uses the granted Gmail access solely to:
• Send emails with signed documents on your behalf
• Download and process email attachments when requested
• No data is used for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond the core
functionality
5. Data Storage
• All document processing occurs locally on your computer
• We do not store your emails, documents, or Gmail access tokens on any
external servers
• OAuth tokens are stored securely on your local device only
6. Third-Party Services
Our Application interfaces with Google's Gmail service. Please refer to Google's Privacy
Policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy) for information on how Google handles
your data.
7. Your Rights
You have the right to:
• Revoke access to your Gmail account at any time
• Request information about how your data is being used
• Contact us with any privacy concerns
8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes
by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
